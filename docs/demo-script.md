# Demo Script

1. Open landing page and state the thesis: every sale becomes financial evidence.
2. Login as `admin@zbvaka.co.zw`.
3. Show judge dashboard:
   total merchants, processed activity, verified vs declared evidence mix, allocation split, trust ladder, fraud console.
4. Switch to merchant login `tariro@zbvaka.co.zw`.
5. Open wallet page and explain:
   hybrid evidence capture, weighted trust score, raw trust inputs, and automatic pocket balances.
6. Open receive-money page and show the merchant QR.
7. Run a customer simulator payment for `$20.00`.
8. Show the deterministic split between operating, savings, and growth.
9. Show the digital receipt with evidence type `Verified QR`.
10. Record a cash sale from the cash-sale tab.
11. Show the updated wallet, transaction history badges, and trust score explanation.
12. Mention that verified QR carries higher confidence than merchant-declared cash.
13. Mention that no real rails are integrated yet; the architecture is ready for adapters.
